What are Geographical Indicators?

A geographical indication (GI) is a sign used on products that have a specific geographical origin and possess qualities or a reputation that are due to that origin. In order to function as a GI, a sign must identify a product as originating in a given place.




Why are they important?

Geotagged products are important because they enable businesses to tailor their offerings based on specific geographical locations, allowing for targeted marketing, improved customer engagement, supply chain optimization, compliance with regulations, and enhanced security measures.
